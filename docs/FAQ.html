Membership

What types of memberships do you offer?

We offer a variety of memberships including monthly, annual, and day passes. Each membership type grants access to our facilities and amenities.

Can I try out the gym before committing to a membership?

Yes, we offer a complimentary trial session for all first-time visitors. You can experience our facilities and services before making a commitment.

Do you offer discounts for students, seniors, or other groups?

Yes, we offer discounts for students, seniors, military personnel, and other qualifying groups. Please inquire at our front desk for more information.

What is the duration of your membership contracts?

Our membership contracts typically range from 1 month to 1 year, depending on your preference and needs.

Gym Information

What are your gym's operating hours?

Our gym is open from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM, Monday through Saturday, and from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM on Sundays.

Services

Do you offer personal training services?

Yes, we have certified personal trainers available to help you reach your fitness goals. You can book sessions with them at your convenience.

Are group fitness classes included in my membership?

Yes, group fitness classes are included in most of our membership packages. Check with our staff for specific details.

Do you provide nutritional guidance or dietary plans?

Yes, we offer nutritional guidance and can provide dietary plans tailored to your fitness goals. Our staff can assist you in creating a customized nutrition plan.
